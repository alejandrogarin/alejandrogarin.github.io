Are you a listener of Audiria Spanish Podcasts?

Now, you can have an application that will let you listen to all those great podcasts from your iPhone, iPod or iPad!

Each podcast contains examples and explanations to help you improve your knowledge of the Spanish language across a wide range of topics. If you are new to Audiria, please check out the official web site for more information.

iAudiria app is easy to use application that will let you:

  • See the podcast FULL spanish transcript while listening.
  • Search for podcasts using a filter (over all podcasts or listened podcasts) so you can search for any topic you are interested.
  • Keep track of what you've listened.
  • More than 600 spanish podcasts to choose.
  • Remember where you ended playing any podcast so you can go back later and continue listening to it without necessarily starting over again.
  • No extra space required. You don't need to download the podcast, just play it right away.
  • Easily update to the latest podcasts feed.
  • Continue listening while the app is in the background.
